Welcome To Royal Diadem Church

At RCCG Royal Diadem Parish, destinies are rewritten in accordance with the will of Yahweh, the word of God and the lifestyle of Jesus Christ. Our will is to worship God in spirit and in truth. Our worship is organised in order to offer God an acceptable worship each time we meet. We empower our members to know the true God and to do warfare against forces that limit the realisation of their destinies. Our team of Lifesavers give welfare support to all our needy members regardless of their level of faith and contributions to the Church.
Our Senior Pastors are Pastor Wale Adeduro, Ph.D (aka Pastor WAO) and Pastor (Mrs) Kenny Wale-Adeduro. Pastor WAO is also the Pastor in Charge of the Redeemed Christian Church of God,
