ABOUT US

Welcome to RCCG Royal Diadem Parish. We are known as the King's Palace and the Headquarters Parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Lagos Province 76, Isolo.

OUR VISION

We are committed to the vision of making heaven and taking as many people as possible with us to heaven. Holiness is our lifestyle.

  • We WORSHIP the almighty God in the beauty of holiness.
  • We engage in spiritual WARFARE to edify our members and give them access to enjoy their spiritual heritage in Jesus Christ.
  • We pursue the wellbeing of our members and community through WELFARE by caring, providing for them.
  • We are a community that cares about nurturing her members' Dreams and creatively empowering them to becoming result oriented individuals.
  • We help our members and stakeholders to achieve Productivity through intimacy with God reflected by obeying all the revealed instructions of God through His word.

■OUR BELIEFS■
Our belief system is anchored on our irreversible belief in the concepts of trinity, incarnation, the death and

resurrection of Jesus Christ, love, salvation, holiness, the word of God which is the Bible, prayers, confession of

sins, repentance as well as forgiveness, second coming of Jesus Christ, rapture, restitution, bride of Christ, the

sanctity of the marriage between one man and one woman etc.
